Dice Game

How To Play

Each player recieves two dice for each round consisting of three rounds for the entire game.

For each round, the player with the highest value of dice wins the round.

At the end of the third round the the player with the most win, wins the entire game.

Rules:

  • If any player's two dice produce a 1, that player receives a score of 0 for that round. For instance, if the player rolls a 6 and a 1, they receive a score of 0.
  • If any player rolls a pair of the same numbers, their score is equal to the sum of the two dice multiplied by 2. For instance, if the player rolls a 5 and a 5, their score is (5+5)*2=20 .
  • If the player rolls any other combination of dice than those listed above, their score will be equal to the sum of the two dice, for example, if they roll a 3 and a 2, their score is 3+2=5 .
